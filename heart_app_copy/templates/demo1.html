How to Use the Heart Disease Predictor

Enter your health details in the form on the right to predict the likelihood of heart disease. The model uses several health metrics such as:

  • Age
  • Sex (Gender)
  • Chest Pain Type
  • Resting Blood Pressure
  • Cholesterol
  • Fasting Blood Sugar
  • Resting ECG
  • Maximum Heart Rate
  • Exercise-Induced Angina
  • Oldpeak (ST depression)
  • ST Slope

Once all the required fields are filled, submit the form to get the prediction results.
